Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access my AI assistant?

After subscribing, you'll receive a unique access link and auth token. Open the link in any browser (desktop or mobile) and start chatting. No app install required.

Where is my data stored?

Your data is stored in a private Google Spreadsheet created just for you. You have full access to view and export your data at any time.

What counts as a "conversation"?

Each message you send to the assistant counts as one conversation. You get 100 per month, which resets on the 1st of each month.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel through your payment portal at any time. Your data remains accessible in your Google Spreadsheet even after cancellation.

Is my data shared with other users?

No. Each subscriber gets their own isolated spreadsheet. Your formulas, tasks, and conversations are completely private.

What AI model powers this?

Our assistants are powered by Anthropic's Claude AI, known for being helpful, harmless, and honest. We use specialized tool-calling to interact with your data accurately.
